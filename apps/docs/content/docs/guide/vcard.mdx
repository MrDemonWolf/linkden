# vCard

Give visitors a one-tap way to save your contact info. When enabled, a download button appears on your public page that saves a `.vcf` file compatible with iOS, Android, macOS, and Windows.

## Enabling vCard

1. Go to `/admin/settings`.
2. Find the **vCard** section.
3. Toggle it on.

A download button will appear on your public page.

## Filling In Your Details

Edit your vCard fields in the vCard section of settings. Only fields you fill in are included in the downloaded file -- everything else is left out automatically.

### Personal Information

- **Full Name** -- Your name as it should appear in contacts.
- **Nickname** -- An alternative name or handle.
- **Birthday** -- Your birthday.
- **Photo** -- Profile photo URL.

### Professional Information

- **Organization** -- Company or organization name.
- **Title** -- Your role or position.
- **Department** -- Department within the organization.

### Contact Details

- **Email** -- Personal email address.
- **Work Email** -- Work email address.
- **Phone** -- Personal phone number.
- **Work Phone** -- Work phone number.

### Address

Your full mailing address.

### URLs

Add links to your websites, social profiles, or any other URLs. Each entry has a label and a URL.

## Format

LinkDen generates vCard 3.0 files, which have the widest compatibility across devices and apps.
