# Welcome to LinkDen

Create a fast, beautiful link-in-bio page that you fully own and control. LinkDen runs on your own infrastructure, gives you a polished admin panel, and loads instantly for visitors worldwide.

**Your links, your brand, your rules.**

## Why LinkDen?

- **Blazing fast** -- Pages load in under a second, served from the edge.
- **Full admin panel** -- Manage blocks, appearance, analytics, vCard, and Apple Wallet passes from one dashboard.
- **7 built-in themes** -- Each with dark and light variants (14 total) plus auto mode.
- **5 block types** -- Links, headers, social icons, embeds, and contact forms.
- **100+ social brands** -- Searchable icon library with automatic dark-mode support.
- **Banner system** -- Pick from preset gradients or upload your own image.
- **Apple Wallet** -- Generate signed `.pkpass` files for iPhone contact cards.
- **Whitelabel** -- Remove all LinkDen branding with a single toggle. MIT licensed.
- **Export/Import** -- Back up and restore all your data as JSON.
- **Secure by default** -- XSS protection, URL validation, CSS sanitization, and a one-time setup token.
